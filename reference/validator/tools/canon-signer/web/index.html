    What am I looking at?

    A Canon fact is a COSE_Sign1 envelope over a 7-field CBOR array — a tiny, self-describing record that carries its own cryptographic signature. This page downloads a WebAssembly build of Canon's own canon-verify verifier, runs it inside your browser, and shows every single step it takes.

    The signing side is deliberately not in this bundle. If someone ever managed to convince this page to emit a valid signature, the audit surface is ephemeral-crypto and not the 250 KB of JavaScript you downloaded a moment ago.
